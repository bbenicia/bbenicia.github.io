The Cutty Board

Educational Kid's Cutting Board

An educational tool in the form of a cutting board that facilitates a learning opportunity for the child to learn cutting techniques to cut typical food and cook with a guardian

Areas
Rapid Prototyping , Consumer Products , Design Engineering
Skills & Tools
Laser Cutter, Belt Sander, SolidWorks, Adobe Illustrator

The Problem

Children need a better way to learn cooking skills as a part of their childhood development in an intuitive, safe, and fun way that gives them agency in learning while also cultivating an opportunity for an older mentor to teach and cook together.

Process
  • Identify Porblem Space
  • User Interviews
  • Competitive Market Analysis
  • Design Requriements
  • Sketching
  • Iterative Prototyping
  • Material Selection
  • Hi-Fi Prototype
    • Research

    Research process included secondary research and 2 x 40 min user interviews. User included 1 current student and 1 current mom of a 5 year old child.

    Target User

    Ages 6+ & Parent,Older Sibling,Mentor,etc

    User Needs
  • Parents desire their child to independently learn while fostering a space for a teaching opportunity.
  • Kids need cooking tools that meet them where they are at
  • Educational tool for children that will streamline to more advanced cooking skills and independent cooking necessary in the later years.
    • Opportunity Space
    Design Requirements
    Ideation
    Iterative Prototyping
    Design Features
  • transparent board ( Polystyrene)
  • Curved white edges
  • sturdy
  • Openings along cutting board to allow easy cleaning
  • Cutting board slot to house inserts for easy storage
  • Polypropolyene Insert kept inside or take out and use as a second cutting board
  • Tabs for easy organization and for easily switching them in and out of slot
  • 5 Different Guidelines that teaches dicing , Batonnet , slices, wedges
  • Dual color to emphasize cutting guidelines
  • Whiteboard insert to allow users to write their own
