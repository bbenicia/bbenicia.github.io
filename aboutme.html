What is a Critical Maker?

Critical maker stemming from critical making , creates computational expierences that critically explore social and culturally relevant themes. It extendes critical thinking , normally presented in abstract linguistic terms, to making , something physical and hands on. I have extended and redrawn this definition to entail activism , design and education. Explore some projects below.
