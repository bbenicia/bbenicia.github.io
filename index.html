Theo Hand

Hyper Personal Prosthetic

Theo Hand is a prosthetic device that utilizes soft robotics to help our Project Partner Theo improve his mobility and way of life. Theo is a 3 year old boy born with only one-third of his right forearm. Because of Theo’s young age and lack of experience with prostheses, the user experience of the device was a huge priority.

The prosthetic functions by turning a knob that tightens the prosthetic into a flexible grip.The device also has LEDs and a speaker that signaled different lights or sounds as haptic feedback to convey that an object was firmly grasped.

Destroy the World in a Day Simulator

Interactive Education Arcade Game

Destroy the World in a Day simulator is an interactive game inspired by 80’s arcade games and choose your own adventure games. This game is focused on educating individuals about sustainability through the lens of everyday life decisions.

Players go through a typical day making decisions about what to eat, how to dress, etc. and see how their decisions immediately impact the environment and the social , political , and economic entanglements of each repercussion. As each arcade buttons were pressed, the color of the neopixel ring each time indicated the health bar of the planet.

Finding Cuby

Autonmous Robot Cube

Finding Cuby is a robot cube that visually detects another cube based on color and autonomously flips towards it in order to attach.The cube has no external moving parts,but instead functions by using a simple mode of locomotion (pivoting), an inertial actuator (a flywheel and brake) and a bonding mechanism that enables it to attach itself to other Cubys (permanent magnets).The potential of the product expands on ideas of macroscopic self assembly and modular structures.

Skeletoids

Interactive Macroscopic Educational Models

Skeletoids are macroscopic models that aim to create an interactive educational experience that would accurately depict the self assembly of peptoids

A Solo project I worked on under the mentorhsip of Ronald Zuckermann for the Molecular Foundary. Design process entailed ideating models and mechanical formation , modeling on CAD software, creating unique key slot joints , 3-D printing, tolerancing , prototyping and iterating .

Toxic Tie

Speculative Wearable

The Toxic Tie is a speculative design piece intended to make a statement on misogyny in the workplace. It recognizes when misogynistic words or phrases are spoken and reacts by folding up and tapping the wearer on the face to inform the wearer that they need to consider the impact of their words. Additionally, it rewards the wearer for saying something complimentary.

The accompanying website allows women to input misogynistic phrases that they have heard in the workplace to help train our tie to better react in these situations. By showing them a a bulletin of all of the crowdsourced phrases creates a sense of collective empathy.

Donde Estoy

Multi Media Art Provocation

Donde Estoy is a multimedia art piece created as a part of my master's program orientation at Northwestern. The theme for this year was Alice in Wonderland, and I chose to center the story's themes of liminality, uncertainty, and isolation. I drew alot of parallels to border crossing and my parents stories of immigrating to the U.S from Mexico. I wanted to convey such a broad concept as border crossing by imagining my mom as alice going down the rabbit hole as she crosses the mexico us border.The main messages I wanted to center were language barriers and the struggle of sacrificing an old life in order to have new opportuniites.I wanted to focus on showcasing narratives, like my parents , that aren't told and centered.

Plant-E

Autonomous Plant Care System

Plant-E is a lovable and commercial autonomous watering and care plant system aimed for busy college students who are notorious for killing their plants.

I created this solo project for my IoT class.I programmed this IoT device using Arduino Uno to take sensing data and send it to the cloud to show on a mobile app and twitter as well as to actuate a water valve and notify users of water levels
