Peanut Robotics


(Professional) Berkeley Innovation
Fall 2018

In an interdiscplnary team of 4 with Berkeley Innovation we worked with Peanut Robotics , a starup at SkyDeck . We were tasked with conceptualizing the industrial design of a hotel cleaning robot for a start-up company by conducting user research and taking into account social robotic principles and placement of tools for optimal cleaning path following a human centered design cycle

I conducted user research and visualized feedback by creating a user journey and heat map to determine pain points of cleaning staff that the robot would alleviate and interactions

I delivered detailed 3D models and multiple renderings for possible industrial design of robot
