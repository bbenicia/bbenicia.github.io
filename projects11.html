Innovating Hair Cleansing with P&G

Human Centered Consumer Product Design

As a part of my graduate course Human Centered Product Design Studio at Northwestern, my team was given a brief by our project client P&G Heaad & Shoulders in order to innovate the hair cleansing expierence for dandruff sufferers.


This project is currently under NDA and therfore some aspects of the process and final product are disclosed for confidentiality reasons.

    • In Home Interviews

    Before thinking about a solution , it was essential to frame the problem and understand our users and their struggles and needs. In order to empathize with our users my team and I conducted 7 , 1hr in home user interviews.Before each interview,my team and I created a discussion guide and research stimuli for the interview. With mindful observation and thoughtful questions, I was able to empathize with our user and gather stories to for the synthesis phase.

    Synethesis
  • Better understand process of cleaning of a hotel bathroom from start to finish including timeline and and variety oof tools used
  • Explore potential pain points in certain tasks that staff face when cleaning inlcuding hard to reach places, and most time consuming tasks
  • Idenitfy the optimal interaction between staff and robot and positioning with robot and staff.
