Peanut Robotics

Human Centered Industrial Design

As a part of Berkeley Innovation, my team was tasked to conceptualize and design the industrial design of a hotel cleaning robot. We worked with Peanut Robotics, a bay area robotics start up. Berkeley Innnovation is a human centered design consultancy group at UC Berkeley that helps clients ranging from large corporations to start ups to local buisness solve an array of design challenges.

Areas
Industrial Design ,Human Centered Design , Robotics
Skills & Tools

My Role

In an interdiscplanary team of four, I worked on ethnographic research , needfinding, and primarily the modeling and rendering of the hotel cleaning robot.I also led meeting with the client to present progress.
