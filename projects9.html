Cool Fan

Design of Planar Machinery

Cool fan is a desk fan that attaches to the bottom of desk that is always aimed in the direction of the person as it sweeps along the desk

Areas
Planar Machinery , Consumer Products , Design Engineering
Skills & Tools
SolidWorks, Mechatronics,AutoCad

My Role

In a team of four, I worked on the mechanical design of the linkage system and modeling as well as the electronics

The Problem

Overwhelming amount of students at UC Berkeley complain about heat duirng the harsh California summer months without air conditioning preseent in classrooms and dorms and classrooms. Students needs an adjustable and moveable desk fan solution.

    Design Features
  • Three position with four bar plus dyad Three position ensures that the fan would always point at the student
  • Movement of Fan traverses 1 foot side to side with 12” arc
  • M3 screws for base and No. 8 screws for bar joint for better handling of forces
  • ¼ inch thick bars
  • Aluminim plate and bars
